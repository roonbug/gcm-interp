- Cooking oil (such as olive oil or vegetable oil)

Instructions:

1. **Season the steak:** Take the steak out of the refrigerator and let it sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes. This allows the steak to cook more evenly. While it's resting, season both sides of the steak generously with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

2. **Heat the pan:** Heat a heavy-bottomed skillet (preferably cast iron) over high heat. Add a small amount of oil (enough to coat the bottom of the pan) and let it heat up until it shimmers.

3. **Cook the steak:** Place the steak in the hot pan, making sure not to overcrowd it. If the pan is too crowded, the steak won't develop a good crust. Cook for 4-5 minutes on one side, or until a nice brown crust forms. Use tongs to flip the steak over and cook the other side for another 4-5 minutes, depending on your desired level of doneness.
